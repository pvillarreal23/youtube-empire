# EP001 Hook Rewrite — MrBeast DNA Applied

## Current Hook (weak — starts with character, not stakes):
"Sarah Chen's phone buzzed at nine forty-seven AM. Emergency meeting.
By ten AM, half her team was gone."

## Problem:
- Opens with a name nobody knows yet — no reason to care
- Takes 10 seconds to reach the stakes
- Doesn't create a curiosity gap
- No specific promise of what the viewer will learn

---

## REWRITTEN HOOK (MrBeast DNA):

### First 5 Seconds — STAKES:
"Half her team was gone by ten AM. Replaced by AI tools that cost forty-nine dollars a month."

### Seconds 5-15 — CURIOSITY GAP:
"But here's what nobody expected — the people who moved fast saw twenty-eight percent salary increases. The people who froze? Eight months to recover. The difference? A single decision made in the first ninety days."

### Seconds 15-30 — CREDIBILITY + PROMISE:
"I tracked forty-seven people through this exact transformation — across eight industries, four continents. Three survival patterns emerged. And by the end of this video, you'll know exactly which pattern determines whether you thrive or get left behind."

### Second 30 — RE-HOOK INTO FIRST CHARACTER:
"The first pattern: immediate action. Sarah Chen is the clearest example. Three days after the AI announcement, while her colleagues were still in shock, Sarah did something that changed everything."

---

## Why this works:
1. **Stakes in first 2 seconds** — "Half her team was gone" (viewer thinks: could that happen to me?)
2. **Specific numbers** — 49 dollars, 28%, 8 months, 90 days (credibility)
3. **Curiosity gap** — "A single decision" (what decision?? I need to know)
4. **Promise** — "you'll know exactly which pattern determines..." (clear value)
5. **Character earned** — Sarah isn't introduced until the viewer already CARES about the topic
6. **Open loop** — "Sarah did something that changed everything" (what did she do??)

---

## TITLE OPTIONS (MrBeast rules: under 50 chars, curiosity gap):

| Option | Chars | Why it works |
|--------|-------|-------------|
| Half Her Team Was Gone by 10 AM | 32 | Story-driven, immediate stakes |
| The 90-Day Rule Nobody Talks About | 35 | Framework + exclusivity |
| I Tracked 47 People Through the AI Shift | 41 | Credibility + scale |
| She Lost Her Team to AI. Then Tripled Her Salary. | 50 | Transformation arc |
| 3.2 Million People Missed This | 30 | Scale + curiosity |

**Recommended**: "Half Her Team Was Gone by 10 AM" — shortest, most visceral, pure story hook that creates a "what happened??" reaction.

## THUMBNAIL REWRITE:

Current concept: Woman at crossroads (generic, seen on 100 channels)

### New concept (MrBeast rules — 3 elements, phone-readable):
- **Element 1**: Woman's face, looking down at phone, genuinely shocked expression (not stock photo posed)
- **Element 2**: Bold text "10 AM" in large cyan (#00D4FF) 
- **Element 3**: Dark background with faint red "TERMINATED" overlay barely visible

Why: Creates a story moment. "What happened at 10 AM?" + the emotion on her face + hint of termination = irresistible click.
