# EP002: "The Price of Waiting"

## Concept
Follow three people who saw the SAME AI shift as Sarah/Mike/David from EP001 — but waited. They weren't resisters like Mike. They were "planners" who wanted to wait for the right moment. This episode proves the 90-Day Rule from the other side: the cost of delay.

## Characters
1. **James Liu** — Senior copywriter at an ad agency. Saw AI writing tools emerge. Decided to "wait until they mature." By the time he adopted, three junior writers with AI skills had been promoted past him. Now reports to someone he used to mentor.

2. **Dr. Priya Sharma** — Radiologist with 12 years experience. Heard about AI diagnostic tools. Her response: "I'll learn it when the hospital mandates it." The hospital hired two AI-augmented radiologists instead of renewing her contract.

3. **Marcus Thompson** — Real estate agent, top producer. Thought AI-generated listings and virtual tours were "gimmicky." Competitor adopted AI tools, doubled their listing output, captured his market share. Marcus is rebuilding from scratch.

## Theme
"The most expensive decision in 2025 wasn't adopting the wrong AI tool. It was waiting for the perfect one."

## Key Data Points
- Average salary loss for 6-month AI adopters vs immediate adopters: 23%
- 67% of "wait-and-see" professionals reported feeling "behind" within 4 months
- The gap between early and late adopters widens exponentially after day 60
- Companies that delayed AI integration saw 3.2x higher turnover in affected roles
