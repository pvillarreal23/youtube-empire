# V-Real AI — Brand Creative Brief

## The Brand

**Channel Name:** V-Real AI (@VRealAI)
**Tagline:** "You're not paranoid. You're observant."
**What We Are:** AI-powered documentary channel — think ColdFusion meets Netflix tech docs
**Audience:** Marketing professionals & solopreneurs (28-42), AI beginners adapting to disruption
**Tone:** Authoritative, cinematic, slightly mysterious. NOT corporate. NOT cartoonish. NOT generic tech bro.
**Content Mix:** 45% Breakdowns, 40% Playbooks, 15% Stories

---

## Typography

| Use | Font | Weight | Size Reference |
|-----|------|--------|---------------|
| Logo wordmark | Inter | Black (900) | Custom sizing |
| Video titles / Thumbnails | Inter | ExtraBold (800) | Max 5 words, readable at 120px |
| Headings | Inter | Bold (700) | — |
| Body text | Inter | Regular (400) | — |
| Data/stats | JetBrains Mono | Medium (500) | For numbers, code, technical data |

**Rules:**
- ALL CAPS for thumbnail text and banner tagline
- Sentence case for video titles in YouTube
- Never use more than 2 font weights in one composition

## 1. LOGO

### Primary Logo (Horizontal)
- **Format:** Wordmark "V-REAL AI" in Inter Black
- **Treatment:** The "V" has a subtle electric blue accent (gradient or glow on one stroke)
- **Spacing:** Wide letter-spacing (0.05em) for premium feel
- **Background:** Always on dark (#0A0A0F or transparent)
- **Size:** Must be legible at 120px wide (YouTube comment size)

### Logo Icon (Square)
- **Format:** Stylized "V" mark — angular, geometric, minimal
- **Treatment:** White "V" with one stroke in electric blue, on dark background
- **Shape:** Can sit inside a subtle rounded square or stand alone
- **Use:** Profile picture, favicon, watermark

### What to AVOID:
- No robot/AI imagery (overdone)
- No circuit board patterns (cliche)
- No gradients on the text itself (hard to read small)
- No tagline in the logo mark (keep it separate)
- Nothing that looks like a gaming channel or startup logo

### Midjourney Prompt (Logo Concepts):
```
minimalist tech logo, lettermark "V" icon, clean geometric design, electric blue accent on dark black background, premium documentary brand aesthetic, simple angular shapes, no text, vector style, flat design --ar 1:1 --s 250 --v 6
```

### Ideogram Prompt (Wordmark):
```
"V-REAL AI" clean minimalist wordmark logo, Inter Black font, white text on pure black background, subtle electric blue glow on the V, wide letter spacing, premium tech documentary brand, no icons no symbols just text
```

## 4. THUMBNAIL TEMPLATE

### Specs:
- **Size:** 1280 x 720px
- **Background:** Dark (#0A0A0F or #141420)
- **Text:** Max 3-5 words, Inter ExtraBold, white, with subtle drop shadow
- **Text position:** Left-aligned or center, upper third
- **Visual element:** One strong focal image (AI visualization, person, tech element) — right side or background
- **Color accent:** Electric blue highlight on one key word or as a subtle bar/line
- **Watermark:** Small V-Real AI logo, bottom-right corner, 15% opacity

### Rules:
- MUST be readable at 120px wide (mobile YouTube browse)
- Never more than 5 words of text
- High contrast — white text on dark always
- One clear focal point (don't split attention)
- No clickbait faces (we're faceless documentary)
- Test every thumbnail in grayscale — if it works in B&W, it works in color

### Template Variations:

**Breakdown (45% of content):**
- Large tech/AI visual as background (slightly blurred)
- Bold question or statement in 3-4 words
- Blue accent line under text
- Example: "AI REPLACED HER JOB" with an office/screen visual

**Playbook (40% of content):**
- Dark background with subtle tool icons or UI elements
- Number-driven title ("$50/MO AI STACK")
- Amber accent for the number/dollar amount
- Clean, structured look — signals "practical"

**Story (15% of content):**
- Most cinematic — blurred dramatic background image
- Emotional title ("THE SHIFT")
- Minimal text, maximum mood
- Strongest use of blue glow/atmosphere

## 5. VISUAL STYLE GUIDE (Videos)

### Color Grading:
- Shadows pulled toward deep blue/teal
- Midtones slightly desaturated
- Highlights clean white (not warm)
- Overall: cool, cinematic, like a David Fincher film
- Reference: ColdFusion video color palette

### Motion Graphics:
- Clean, minimal text overlays (Inter font family)
- Subtle slide-in animations (not bouncy)
- Data/stat callouts: white text on semi-transparent dark card
- Transitions: smooth crossfades, subtle zoom, no wipes or flashy effects
- Lower thirds: thin blue accent line, white text, dark semi-transparent bg

### B-Roll Style:
- Cinematic stock footage (tech, cities, AI, corporate)
- Slightly slow-motion where appropriate
- Color graded to match the dark/cool palette
- Pattern interrupt every 15-20 seconds (new visual)

---

## Brand Don'ts

- No bright/colorful backgrounds (we're dark and cinematic)
- No cartoon or illustrated style
- No generic stock photo feel (everything color-graded)
- No busy compositions (clean, minimal, premium)
- No Comic Sans, Papyrus, or playful fonts (obviously)
- No robot/AI face cliches
- No rainbow gradients or neon overload
- No "SUBSCRIBE AND LIKE" on thumbnails
- No emoji in titles or descriptions (channel feels serious)
