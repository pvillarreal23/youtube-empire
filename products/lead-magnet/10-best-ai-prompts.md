### 1. The "Rewrite This Better" Prompt
```
Rewrite this to be clearer, more professional, and half the length.
Keep my main point but make it sound like someone who knows what
they're talking about:

[paste your text here]
```
**Use it for:** Emails you've been staring at for 20 minutes. Slack messages that sound wrong. LinkedIn posts that feel awkward.

---

### 2. The "Explain It Simply" Prompt
```
Explain [topic] to me like I'm intelligent but have zero background
in this subject. Use one real-world analogy. Keep it under 200 words.
No jargon.
```
**Use it for:** Learning new concepts fast. Preparing to talk about something you don't fully understand yet. Impressing people at meetings.

---

### 3. The "5-Minute Meeting Prep" Prompt
```
I have a meeting about [topic] with [who] in [time].
My role is [your role]. Help me prepare:
1. 3 smart questions to ask
2. Key points I should bring up
3. Anything I should watch out for
4. A 30-second summary of my position if asked
```
**Use it for:** Every meeting. Especially the ones you forgot about until 10 minutes ago.

---

### 4. The "Make a Decision" Prompt
```
I'm trying to decide between [option A] and [option B].
Here's my situation: [context].
Give me a clear pros/cons comparison and tell me which one you'd
pick if you were in my shoes. Be direct.
```
**Use it for:** Career decisions, purchases, strategy calls, anything where you're going in circles.

---

### 5. The "Messy Notes to Clean Doc" Prompt
```
Here are my rough notes from [meeting/brainstorm/research].
Organize them into a clean document with headers and bullet points.
Group related ideas. Flag action items.

[paste messy notes]
```
**Use it for:** After every meeting. After every brainstorm. After every voice memo you rambled into your phone.

---

### 6. The "Marketing Plan in 10 Minutes" Prompt
```
Create a 30-day marketing plan for [your business].
Budget: [amount or "zero — organic only"]
Target audience: [who]
Goal: [followers / sales / leads]
Give me specific daily/weekly actions, not vague advice.
```
**Use it for:** When you know you need to market but don't know where to start.

---

### 7. The "Fix My Writing" Prompt
```
Review this and improve it. Don't rewrite entirely — preserve my
voice. Just fix: awkward phrasing, unnecessary words, weak verbs,
and unclear sentences.

[paste your draft]
```
**Use it for:** Blog posts, proposals, emails, anything where you want to sound like yourself but better.

---

### 8. The "Build an AI Workflow" Prompt
```
I do [this task] every [frequency] and it takes me [time].
Design an AI-powered workflow that automates or speeds this up.
Be specific: which tools, what order, what I still do manually.
Estimate time saved. Keep it realistic for one person.
```
**Use it for:** Finding the 20% of your work that AI can handle so you focus on the 80% that matters.

---

### 9. The "Business Idea Gut Check" Prompt
```
I have a business idea: [describe it in 2-3 sentences].
Act as a skeptical but fair advisor. Give me:
1. Strongest argument FOR this idea
2. Strongest argument AGAINST it
3. Who the ideal customer is
4. What to test first before spending money
5. An honest 1-10 rating on viability
```
**Use it for:** Before you invest time or money in anything. Get the brutal feedback first.

---

### 10. The "Prompt Fixer" (Meta-Prompt)
```
I want to [goal]. I keep getting mediocre results from AI.
Act as a prompt engineering expert. Ask me 5 clarifying questions,
then write me the perfect prompt. Explain why each part matters.
```
**Use it for:** When nothing else works. This prompt teaches AI to help you use AI better.
