Step 2 — Scale all footage to 1920x1080 and trim to needed duration:
For each scene file, run:
ffmpeg -i scene_XX_name.mp4 -vf "scale=1920:1080:force_original_aspect_ratio=decrease,pad=1920:1080:(ow-iw)/2:(oh-ih)/2" -t DURATION scene_XX_scaled.mp4

Scene durations (seconds): 12, 12, 21, 37, 23, 40, 35, 30, 45, 45, 40, 35, 75, 90

Step 3 — Concatenate all scaled scenes:
Create scenes.txt:

file 'scene_02_scaled.mp4'
... (all 14 scenes)

ffmpeg -f concat -safe 0 -i scenes.txt -c:v libx264 -crf 18 ep001-video-concat.mp4

Step 4 — Mix voice + music (music ducked to -28 LUFS under voice):
ffmpeg -i ep001-voice-normalized.mp3 -i ep001-music-main.mp3 -filter_complex "[1:a]loudnorm=I=-28:TP=-1[music];[0:a][music]amix=inputs=2:duration=first:weights=1 0.3[out]" -map "[out]" ep001-audio-mixed.mp3

Step 5 — Merge video + audio:
ffmpeg -i ep001-video-concat.mp4 -i ep001-audio-mixed.mp3 -c:v copy -c:a aac -b:a 192k -shortest ep001-the-shift-final.mp4
