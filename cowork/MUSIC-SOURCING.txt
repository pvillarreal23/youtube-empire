V-Real AI needs background music for documentary episodes. The music creates the emotional foundation — without it, the video is just a voice over stock footage.

REQUIREMENTS:
- Royalty-free / copyright-safe for YouTube monetization
- Dark, cinematic, documentary tone
- Low BPM (70-90) — thoughtful, not energetic
- No lyrics
- Minimum 3 minutes (gets looped to fill episode)
- Downloadable as MP3 or WAV

SOURCES TO TRY (in order):
1. Pixabay Music (pixabay.com/music) — 100% free, no attribution needed
   Search: "dark cinematic documentary" or "ambient technology"
   Download the best match

2. YouTube Audio Library (studio.youtube.com → Audio Library)
   Filter: Genre = Cinematic, Mood = Dark/Dramatic
   These are pre-cleared for YouTube use

3. Uppbeat (uppbeat.io) — free tier available
   Search: "documentary" or "cinematic dark"

WHAT TO DOWNLOAD FOR EP001:
- 1 main background track (dark, cinematic, building tension)
- 1 emotional/reflective track (for Sarah/Mike/David stories)
- 1 hopeful/rising track (for the framework/solution section)

SAVE FILES TO:
Upload to Google Drive folder: VReal-AI/music/
Name them:
- ep001-bg-main.mp3
- ep001-bg-emotional.mp3
- ep001-bg-hopeful.mp3

The video assembler will automatically duck the music under the voiceover (drops from -28 LUFS to -35 LUFS when voice is active).
