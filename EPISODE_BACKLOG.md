## Scoring Formula

Each concept scored on 5 factors (weighted):
- **Search Demand** (25%) — Would people Google this?
- **Audience Match** (25%) — Relevant to ambitious professionals 25-45?
- **Retention Potential** (20%) — Strong story arc? Emotional weight?
- **Timeliness** (15%) — Happening now? News cycle relevant?
- **Competition Density** (15%) — How many similar videos exist?

Minimum score to approve: **7.5/10**
