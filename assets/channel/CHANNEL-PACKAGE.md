## Channel Description (About Page)
AI is changing everything — your job, your industry, your income. Most people feel it coming but don't know what to do about it.

V-Real AI is a cinematic documentary series that tracks real people navigating the AI transformation. We follow marketers, developers, lawyers, creatives, and entrepreneurs as they adapt (or don't) to a world where AI can do 80% of what they used to get paid for.

Every episode is researched, scripted, narrated, and produced using AI tools — transparently. We show exactly what we used and how, so you can apply the same techniques.

This channel is for:
- Marketing professionals figuring out AI
- Solopreneurs building AI-powered businesses  
- Anyone who wants to understand what's coming — before their 90 days are up

New documentary episodes every Tuesday.

Made by Pedro Villarreal.
