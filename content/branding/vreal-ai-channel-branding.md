# V-Real AI — Channel Branding Brief

## Brand Identity

**Channel Name:** V-Real AI
**Tagline:** Build with AI. Own your future.
**Niche:** AI agents, automation, building income with AI
**Tone:** Real, direct, documentary-style, no fluff
**Audience:** People who feel the AI shift and want to act — not developers, not tech bros, regular people who are ready to learn

## Color Palette

- **Primary:** #8b5cf6 (Purple — stands out, techy but accessible)
- **Secondary:** #0f172a (Dark slate — dark mode energy, serious)
- **Accent:** #10b981 (Green — growth, money, go)
- **Text:** #e2e8f0 (Light gray on dark backgrounds)
- **Warning/Urgency:** #f59e0b (Amber — for "act now" moments)

## Typography

- **Headlines:** Bold, sans-serif (Inter, Geist, or Montserrat)
- **Body:** Clean sans-serif
- **Accent text:** Monospace for anything code/tech related

## Profile Picture

**Concept:** Minimalist "V" icon that combines the V-Real brand with AI circuitry

**Option A — Geometric V:**
- Clean geometric "V" shape
- Purple gradient (#8b5cf6 → #6d28d9)
- Subtle circuit-board pattern inside the V
- Dark background (#0f172a)
- Works at tiny sizes (32px favicon through 800px profile)

**Option B — V + Pulse:**
- "V" with an AI "pulse" line running through it (like a heartbeat/signal)
- Purple on dark
- Suggests "alive" and "real" — fitting the "V-Real" name

**Option C — Lettermark:**
- Bold "VR" stacked or side-by-side
- "AI" in smaller text below or beside
- Purple accent, dark background

**Recommended:** Option A — simplest, most recognizable at small sizes

## Thumbnail Style Guide

**Consistent elements across all videos:**
- Dark background (matches channel brand)
- Bold white or yellow text (max 3-4 words)
- Purple accent color for highlights
- High contrast — must pop at mobile size
- No faces for now (text + graphic style)
- Bottom-right corner: small "V-Real AI" watermark

**Template variations:**
1. **"The Breakdown"** — Large stat/number + topic text
2. **"The Tutorial"** — Tool icon/screenshot + action text
3. **"The Warning"** — Amber/red accent + urgent text
4. **"The System"** — Flowchart/diagram style showing a process

## Tools for Creating These

- **Profile pic + banner:** Canva, Figma, or AI image generation (Midjourney/DALL-E)
- **Thumbnails:** Canva templates matching the style guide above
- **Automated thumbnails:** Use the Thumbnail Designer agent + Canva API via Make.com
